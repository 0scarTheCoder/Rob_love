About Associate Professor Robert Love

Leading Plastic & Reconstructive Surgeon

Associate Professor Robert Love is a highly respected plastic and reconstructive surgeon with over two decades of surgical excellence. He graduated with honors from the University of Melbourne in 1991 and achieved Fellowship of the Royal Australasian College of Surgeons (FRACS) in 2001.

Leadership & Recognition

  • Past Chairman of West Australian regional committee, Royal Australian College of Surgeons
  • Immediate past Head of Plastic Surgery at Sir Charles Gairdner Hospital
  • Past President of West Australian Society of Plastic Surgeons
  • Leibinger Travelling Fellowship prize winner (awarded twice)
  • Rotary Paul Harris Fellowship recipient
  • Special Services Award from Royal Australian College of Surgeons
